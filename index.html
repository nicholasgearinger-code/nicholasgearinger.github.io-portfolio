Requires camera access. Nothing is recorded or sent anywhere — detection runs 100% locally in your browser.

faces detected: 0

Each toggle loads its own model on demand, the first time you switch it on — nothing downloads until you ask for it. Running several at once is more demanding on your device, so if things feel sluggish, try enabling fewer at a time.

Turn on Hands and a small 3D model appears in the corner — pick which one from the dropdown, move your hand to orbit it, and pinch your thumb and index finger together to zoom in and out.

Turn on Body Pose and a jointed character appears in the opposite corner, built live in Three.js — its shoulders, elbows, hips, and knees are driven directly by your own joint angles. It tracks motion across the camera's view (raise an arm, bend a knee) but not toward/away from the camera, since a single 2D camera can't recover that depth on its own.

Face + expression + landmarks: face-api.js. Objects: COCO-SSD. Hands & body pose: TensorFlow.js's official MediaPipe-based models. All MIT/Apache licensed, all inference client-side.
