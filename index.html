How I Work

A process built for
intelligent products

AI projects fail when engineering and design work in separate rooms. My process treats them as the same discipline from day one.

01

Discover the real problem

User interviews, technical audits, and AI opportunity mapping — before any wireframe exists.

02

Define the AI's role

Not every feature needs a model. I map where AI adds genuine value versus where it adds friction.

03

Design and prototype in tandem

Figma prototypes and live model outputs evolve side-by-side so design assumptions get tested early.

04

Ship, measure, iterate

I stay through launch and beyond — tracking behaviour, retraining where needed, and closing the loop.
